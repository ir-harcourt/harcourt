Featured Products

Headed Slotted Locator Bushings

An innovative range of components similar to our standard Slotted Locator Bushings but with a head to retain the part in the tool. These parts ensure location repeatability and prevent FOD issues. For more information and CAD check out the 'Locators' section of our online catalog & CAD library.

SMR Drift Nests

Drift Nests are temporary, magnetic SMR mounts for almost any location and surface. They can be used repeatedly, as they are mounted with any general adhesive such as a hot glue gun. Made from 7075-T6 aluminum with a special hard coat these drift nests are very durable making them the ideal choice for SMR nesting. For more information and CAD check out the 'Locator' section of our online catalog & CAD library".
