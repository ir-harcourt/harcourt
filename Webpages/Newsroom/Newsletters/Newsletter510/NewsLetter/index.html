TECHNICAL HELP

Having trouble opening our CATIA V5 models?

In order to support all revisions of CATIA version 5, Harcourt CATIA V5 files are delivered in an archived folder (.zip extension) as CATIA Macro files (.CATscript extension). For quick instructions on opening these files please click here. For any other technical issues or assistance please email us.

DESIGNING DRILL JIGS?

We have the answer to power-feed liner bushings!

Be sure to check out our popular Helix Lock Liners. These airfeed lock liners incorporate a 6 degree helix angle in the locking tabs which securely locks a power-feed drilling unit. Not only are vibrations greatly reduced but they also ensure comfort for the user and top quality in the finished tool. For more information on this product check out our Drill Bushing Catalog and CAD Configurator.
